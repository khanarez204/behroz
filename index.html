Our Story

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

'98

Behroz Group Founded

Started as a small family dairy operation in Chakdara, Lower Dir, with a single mission: deliver pure, honest produce.

'04

Seafood Division Launched

Expanded into aquaculture, bringing fresh river fish, prawns and shellfish to local markets for the first time.

'10

Faizan Hardware & Agri Machinery Established

Answered the call from local farmers for reliable tractors, irrigation pumps and industrial hardware.

'16

Peshawar Sub-Office Opens

Opened our Hayatabad facility to better serve Peshawar and the wider Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region.

'21

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Formalized our quality management systems across every division, audited to international standards.

Today

Serving over 10,000 clients across 150+ product lines, with the same family values we started with.
